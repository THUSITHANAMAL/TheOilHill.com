How to Calm a
Worried, Busy, or Troubled Mind

Utilize the spiritual and aromatic properties of the MT Anointing Oil to find peace and tranquility during moments of stress or anxiety.

To find peace and tranquility during moments of stress or anxiety, you can utilize the spiritual and aromatic properties of the MT Anointing Oil. This method combines prayer, faith, and the soothing aroma of the oil to help calm your mind and spirit.

Simple Practice

Steps to Use MT Anointing Oil
for Peace of Mind

1

Prepare the Oil

Take 5-7 drops of the MT Anointing Oil and apply them onto a clean, folded tissue or Kleenex. This allows the aroma to be concentrated and easily inhaled.

2

Recite the 23rd Psalm

While holding the tissue, recite the 23rd Psalm, a scripture known for its comforting and calming effect. The Psalm begins with "The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want," and emphasizes God's guidance and protection.

3

Place in Your Pillow

After prayer, place the folded tissue between your pillow and pillowcase. Position it at both the right and left ends of the pillow to ensure the fragrance surrounds you throughout the night.

4

Rest and Breathe

As you rest, breathe deeply to absorb the aroma of the oil. The captivating scent will minister to your soul and spirit, providing a sense of divine presence and reassurance.
